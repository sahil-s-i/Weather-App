About Weather-App

Welcome to Weather-App, This Project is go-to source for accurate and up-to-date weather information. The app is designed to provide you with the latest weather forecasts for cities around the world. Whether you're planning a trip, checking the weather for your daily activities, or simply curious about the current conditions, Weather-App has you covered.

Key Features

  • Real-time weather data from trusted sources
  • Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more
  • Search for weather information by city
  • Beautifully designed and user-friendly interface
  • Access weather forecasts for multiple cities

App Functionality

Weather-App fetches weather data from reliable sources to provide you with the most accurate and up-to-date information. Simply enter the name of the city you want to know the weather for, and the app will display detailed weather information, including current temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.

User Guidance

To get started with Weather-App, follow these simple steps:

  1. On the home page, enter the name of the city for which you want to check the weather in the search bar.
  2. Click the "Search" button to retrieve weather information for the specified city.
  3. View the detailed weather data, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.
  4. Explore weather forecasts for multiple cities by searching for different locations.

The App is committed to providing you with accurate and reliable weather information to help you plan your day. Thank you for choosing Weather-App as your weather companion.
